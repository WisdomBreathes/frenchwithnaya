## What the Business Is
French with Naya is a one-to-one online French tutoring service for children from Junior Kindergarten through Grade 5 (roughly ages 4–11). Naya teaches over video call, bringing French to life through age-appropriate methods — songs, stories, games, conversation, and visual learning. Every session is tailored to the child. Parents see results. Kids look forward to it.

## Who Actually Visits the Website
**The parents.** Not the kids. A parent — most likely a mom or dad of a child in JK–Grade 5 — lands on this site while searching for French tutoring for their child. They are:
- Concerned their child is falling behind in French immersion, or
- Wanting a head start before school begins, or
- Looking for supplemental support alongside school, or
- Simply wanting their child to grow up bilingual

**What they feel when they arrive:** A mix of hope and skepticism. They've probably tried apps, workbooks, or a big tutoring company that felt impersonal. They want someone who is warm, qualified, and genuinely good with kids. They're handing over their child's time and their own money — they need to trust Naya before they book.

**What they need to see:** That Naya is real, warm, skilled, and experienced with young children. That the sessions are fun enough that kids won't resist. That there's a clear method, not just improvised conversation.

## Format
- 1:1 sessions only (no group classes)

- Students: JK through Grade 5 (ages ~4–11)

Warm. Nurturing. A little playful. Reassuring without being salesy. Professional enough to earn parent trust, human enough to feel personal. Like a favourite teacher, not a tutoring corporation.
